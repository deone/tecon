Tecon Oil Services Limited is an Indigenous Oilfield Service Company incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. For years now giant strides have been taken and new frontiers broken by Tecon in the Oilfield Equipment Rental and Services to the major players in the upstream sector of the Nigerian Oil and Gas industry. As one of the foremost indigenous service providers in the petroleum industry, Tecon has redefined standards of well engineering services through her stock of technically sophisticated equipment and pool of very experienced and professionally sound personnel.
