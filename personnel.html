Casimir C. Maduafokwa

President/Group CEO, Lagos.
Casimir C. Maduafokwa is the Company's president/CEO. He holds a B.Sc degree in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research from Syracuse University, Syracuse, New York; and MBA degree in Finance & Economics also from Syracuse University.

He started his professional career with Center for Management & Policy Research, Washington DC as a Policy Research Analyst in 1979. In 1981, he proceeded to Atlanta Office of Mathtech Division of Mathematics Inc., Princeton, New Jersey as Econometrician/Operations Research Analyst. He left the United States of America and between 1983 - 1986, he worked in Chase Merchant Bank, Lagos as Assistant Manager, Credit and Marketing. In 1986, he joined First City Merchant Bank Limited as Manager, Credit and Marketing and left in 1989/90 to establish Cosmocorp Holdings - a company engaged in Strategic Investment and Assets Acquisition business.

He became the President/Chief Executive Officer of Tecon Oil Services Limited in 1991 when Cosmocorp Holdings acquired the company.
